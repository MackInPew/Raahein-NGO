About Us



Welcome to Raahein, a compassionate non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to driving positive transformation across a spectrum of critical areas. Our core mission is to contribute to a better society by addressing a range of pressing issues that affect communities globally. Through our multi-faceted approach, we believe in fostering holistic development and empowerment for everyone.

At Raahein, our vision is a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. We envision vibrant communities that are resilient, informed, and united in their pursuit of progress.

Our Work

Women Empowerment

The seminar, themed "Women Empowerment through Agriculture," equipped local women with knowledge and skills for sustainable farming. Esteemed speakers highlighted the importance of women in rural development and the need for balanced agricultural practices. Resource persons from the Agriculture Department shared insights on organic farming and modern techniques. Ngo Raahein and Naba Nirman Foundation pledged support in marketing women's agricultural products, creating wider market opportunities.

Project Vidyaan

Today, the President of ‘Asom Chatra Parishad’ Trinayan Baruah daa was invited as a guest speaker for an interactive session in Bonda Anchalik Highschool under ‘Project Vidyaan’ of Raahein. We wholeheartedly thank him for his inspiring words and convey our sincere gratitude for being a part of it and sharing his precious time with us.
